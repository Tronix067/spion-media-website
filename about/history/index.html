How It Started

What began as a worldview from the streets has evolved into a grand vision today. My journey, which started with understanding people, ignited a dream to establish a hub for like-minded individuals. I left home at an early age and embarked on a journey to seek the truth, growing much faster than my peers. Media was not just a tool for me; it was the reason for existence. In this world, rules were different, and balances were illogical. 2 + 2 was sometimes 5, sometimes 0. I began thinking like an algorithm: random, illogical, yet natural. I reached a different dimension. It was no longer the work; I had become the work itself. Day and night, this journey became my sole purpose. I developed accounts on social media platforms, managed audiences, and generated income through them. With my earnings, I acquired equipment and took on bigger projects. I earned again and returned to the field, because I loved this cursed game. I had the opportunity to work with large productions and decided to crown my acquired media skills within this company, bringing together those better than me. In this company, no one is worse than anyone else. No one is in anyone's chain of command. Every team member is a specially trained digital agent. These agents possess superhuman mentality; they can see beyond reflecting reality and never look through a single window. I set my eyes on the big media giants. I believe my army of agents will defeat them all. I am the leader. All responsibility is mine. I must use my power to protect the good and prevent it from falling to the evil. I am nobody. I am only a reflection of the system. Here, no one has individual traits. Everyone is replaceable. Either you are the best, or you do not exist. Spion Army does not resemble flashy media companies. We create the trend. We rewrite reality. We rebuild the system.
